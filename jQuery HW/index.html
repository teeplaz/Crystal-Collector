CrystalsCollector!

You will be given a random number to start. There are four crystals. Click on a crystal to add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by making your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. Value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it. Each time the game starts, it changes the values of each crystal.
